Welcome to Switch Point Simulations

At Switch Point Simulations, we specialize in creating top-quality content for Train Simulator Classic 2024, Run8 Train Simulator, and Railroader. Our team of experienced developers and real-life railroaders ensures that our content provides an immersive and realistic experience for everyone.

We offer three different lines of freeware to tailor your needs:

  • Ready to Roll: A balance between simple operation and stunning visual/audible experience.
  • Pro User: Uses advanced setups for an authentic and realistic operating experience.
  • Legacy: Older projects that may lack the visual or audible fidelity of newer releases.
